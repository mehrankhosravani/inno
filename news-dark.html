WIIPA Middle East branch

Due to the expansion of the WIIPA, in order to increase unity and focus on new members, it was necessary to establish regional offices. WIIPA is most active in East Asia, and the Middle East, as a developing region, needs more attention. In this regard, a regional office has been established in Turkey.

WIIPA

A non-profit global organization. In 2010, it was founded by Mr. Hsieh Hsin-Ming. For a decade, WIIPA has been using Taiwan as its headquarter. At the moment, 50 member countries and partners have joined the “WIIPA Family” with the goal of promoting invention, innovation, and intellectual property rights around the globe. Innovers is a new name in the realization of international exhibitions of invention and innovation in the world of Metavars, and based on its mission and leadership in this field, WIIPA has created conditions for holding this event.
